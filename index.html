CodeMasters


This app was a monster. Starting out with a wireframe made with Balsamiq, we then drew up the backend by building out all the logic for the board and the pieces of the game, including how to capture and to move and how to recognize obstructions (other pieces that may be in the way of your move). Afterwards, we stretched our limits on our javascript to move the pieces and update them on the board. All told, this took more than 3 months to build out.
